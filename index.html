About Me

I’m a front-end Web Developer with a big love for details and creativity. My journey into web development actually started by chance during college. I was majoring in Computer Science just because I loved computers, without a clear path in mind. One semester, I had to build a website for a final project. At first, I only did it because it was required, and I struggled while following a YouTube course for HTML, CSS, JavaScript But somewhere along the way, I realized I was enjoying it more than I expected. I found myself lost in the details of styling with CSS, customizing every element, and even choosing images from my favorite movies and shows to make the site truly mine. That’s when I discovered my passion for web development — and I haven’t looked back since.

Since then, I’ve worked mostly on landing pages but I love building complete websites too. What sets me apart is my eye for detail and my ability to adapt to each client’s unique taste while adding my own creative touch. I’m always excited to think outside the box and bring fresh ideas to life.

I’m currently diving deeper into React, Bootstrap, and Tailwind, sharpening my skills every day. My short-term goal is to grow as a Freelancer and my long-term dream is to launch my own web development company. If you’re looking for someone who’s just as invested in your vision as you are, I’d love to work with you. Let’s create something amazing together!
